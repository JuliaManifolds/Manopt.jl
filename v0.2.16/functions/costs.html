\[\frac{\lambda}{2}\sum_{i=0}^{N} d_{\mathcal M}(d_i, c_B(i))^2+ \sum_{i=1}^{N-1}\frac{d^2_2 [ B(t_{i-1}), B(t_{i}), B(t_{i+1})]}{\Delta_t^3}\]

For details on the acceleration approximation, see cost_acceleration_bezier. Note that the BeziÃ©r-curve is given in reduces form as a point on a PowerManifold, together with the degrees of the segments and assuming a differentiable curve, the segmenents can internally be reconstructed.
